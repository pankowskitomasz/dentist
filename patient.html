What to Expect During Your First Visit?

Our dentist will thoroughly examine your mouth, including taking oral photographs and x-rays, and then discuss your results to help you better understand your oral health needs.

We will provide you with written treatment details, estimates, and payment plan options.

Stationary services:

  • Tooth Decay Risk Assessment
  • Gum Disease Evaluation
  • Bite Function Analysis
  • 2D & 3D Digital X-Rays
  • Photographs of your smile, teeth, and gums

Remote services:

  • Online consultations
  • Night help in emergency cases
  • Home visits
